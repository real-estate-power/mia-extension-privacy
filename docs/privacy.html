Mia Extension – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-03

Mia is a Chrome extension that helps real estate agents fill forms using file uploads and AI, via a side panel that works with Centris.

Data we collect and use

  • Selected listing ID: When you select a listing in the extension, its ID is stored locally in your browser so the extension can restore your selection. This stays on your device.
  • Centris Zone: The extension can interact with pages on https://zone.centris.ca when you use it there. Page content is used only to support form-filling and related features in the extension and is not sent to us or to any third party.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or for purposes unrelated to the extension's functionality.
  • We do not collect or store your data on our own servers.

Storage and security

  • Stored data (e.g. selected listing ID) is kept only in Chrome's local storage on your device.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy.
